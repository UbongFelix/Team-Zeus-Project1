About Freshfare Mart

...Redefining your grocery experience

Who we are

Freshfood mart is a platform where raw food materials, fresh food items and other food related services are being handled. We connect sellers of these food items to customers who are in need. Our very unique attribute is that we also suggest to customers food stuff combos and recipes to make any meal of their choice, most especially our native delicacies. We are covering the market in Uyo, Nigeria as there are no online fresh food markets here, unlike in Lagos, Port-Harcourt, etc.

During the Covid 19 Pandemic, a lot of food/food related business had to go out of business because they had no medium of selling their products other than being in a physical location. Freshfare mart is out to provide solution to a problem like this. We are her to aid customers get food items to cook from the comfort of their home without the inconvenience of going to the market. Newbies who do not know what to get or where to find them will be aided on this platform through recommendations.
